HOW WE STARTED


On a warm September afternoon, my business partner (funmi) and I wanted to know a quick, legal, and efficent way to make some quick cash for our basketball team and our senior dues. No scams, no scams. We decided to sell snacks at our school. We were reluctant to sell at first, but it was worth a shot. Little did we know how much revenue we would make in a matter of months. We started from selling a litte bit of stuff a day to selling many items daily. We are proud of this business and we plan to keep it going.


BACKGROUND ON MYSELF: I am currently a senior at Jamaica Gateway to the Sciences highschool. I am an aspiring cardio surgeon who plays both basketball and flag football competitively. I am from the countries Nigeria and Ivory Coast.


OUR PLANS


MISSION: Our mission is to raise enough money to put in the bank for rainy days. It's difficult to sell on a daily basis and meet the needs of each of our customers. You know what they say, they customer is always right! It is not about the money - even though the revenue is great. It is more about providing quality snacks to people at affordable price. The customer's happiness is more important than making money to us believe it or not.


VISION: Our vision is a unique one. We plan on smashing all of our competitors with imaginery hammer. What can we say? Who's better than us? We envisioned ourselves only selling throughout the vicinity of our school, but in a matter of weeks, other schools started phoning us up for our one of a kind snacks.
